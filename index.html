FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting Shree Beauty in Westcliff-on-Sea.

How do I book an appointment at Shree Beauty?
Simply use our online booking form powered by Google Forms — scroll down to the Book section or click "Book Appointment" in the menu. Fill in your details, choose your service and time, and you'll receive an automatic confirmation email instantly. If your appointment is approved, you will receive a message confirming your slot.
Where is Shree Beauty located?
We are at 482 Fairfax Dr, Westcliff-on-Sea, Southend-on-Sea, Essex, SS0 9RN — easily accessible from Southend, Leigh-on-Sea and across Essex.
Is Shree Beauty a ladies-only salon?
Yes! Shree Beauty is a ladies-only beauty parlour, providing a comfortable, private and relaxing environment exclusively for women.
What beauty services does Shree Beauty offer?
We offer gel manicures, gel pedicures, manicures, pedicures, eyebrow threading, waxing (including Brazilian and Hollywood wax), eyelash extensions, eyelash perming & tinting, facials and Indian head massage.
Do I need a patch test before eyelash treatments?
Yes — a patch test is required at least 24 hours before any eyelash extension, perming or tinting treatment. Please mention this when filling in the booking form so we can arrange your patch test appointment.
What are your opening hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday 9:00 am – 7:00 pm, and Sunday 10:00 am – 5:00 pm.
Is Shree Beauty near Southend-on-Sea?
Yes — Shree Beauty is in Westcliff-on-Sea, which is adjacent to Southend-on-Sea. We're a short drive or bus ride from Southend High Street and Southend Central station.
